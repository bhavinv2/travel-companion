About Connecting Desis

Built by the community, for the community

Our Story

Connecting Desis was born from a simple frustration — sending a parent or elderly relative on a long international flight alone, with no one to help if something went wrong. We're a platform built by and for the South Asian diaspora, connecting NRIs and Desi travellers who share similar routes so no one has to travel alone.

Our Mission

To make travel safer, friendlier, and more connected for the global Desi community. Whether you're a student flying abroad for the first time, a family traveling with a toddler, or a senior citizen needing companionship — Connecting Desis is here for you.

Our Values

  • Safety First — All communications are masked. Your email is never shared.
  • Community — We believe in the power of the Desi community to support each other.
  • Trust — We're building a platform based on authentic connections.
